T H E . S I T E__

what__
you're reading it right now

ele mental began in may 1993 as a loose, undefined affiliation of people brought together by their involvement in the (modest) electronic music culture of columbus, ohio.

who__
who's in the cru

we are not a collective in the traditional sense. ele_mental is more about the individuals who have associated themselves with it, and the process through which each of us has come to a greater understanding about what our electronic culture can offer us.

most of us are continuing to explore the vast possibilities of electronic music, whether through production or just spinning records (or both). some of us are deeply engaged in the creation of electronic visual forms and graphic design. some of us are struggling to create stronger links between new ideas, and older art forms. a few of us even throw events to try to put these ideas into practice. but there is no agenda. we just do what we do as individuals, and come together when we need to--through technology, graphic design, donating time or equipment at our events, advice, honest opinions, investment, and so on.

what.was__
where we've been

as an event-throwing entity, ele_mental started with the notion of creating affordable dance music parties with an experimental and artistic edge. over the past 5 years, we have thrown over 30 events--from the biggest mega-raves to some of the smallest, most experimental underground events of the midwest region.

what.will.be__
where to go

this mission continues to be refined, and remains the most visible ele_mental ideal at work, always seeking new ways to bring artistic ideals into the party experience.

21/22 corporation__
our music label and design concept

another high-visibility project is the ongoing 21/22 corporation, a project which began with experimental noise/hip-hop in 1992, and whose first vinyl release was the "reconnaissance" drum'n'bass EP by monochrome in early 1995. several vinyl releases have followed, featuring ele_mental (and ele_related) artists. some of these releases have gained overseas attention, and our experimental, low-budget package designs are a natural articulation of ele_mental ideals.

(23)__
abstractions and extractions

the (23) concept extends ele_mental and 21/22 into more abstract artistic areas such as improvisational performance and installation environments. (23) also exists as an online experiment (in need of update).

think.__
tracing the histories of electronic/experimental music

a more concrete realization of the ele_mental ideal, the think project began as an attempt to reveal the deeper connections that today's electronic and experimental music share with older forms. as more information is absorbed, the project grows.

link__
what do you think?

this site has existed since mid-1996. the WWW has changed so drastically, and yet so many sites continue to lack substance and clarity. our links are compiled to bring us together as part of the global online community, and connect us with other sites of depth, simplicity, or personal connection to us.

W H A T . E L S E . M A T T E R S ?
a few of our preoccupations

__minimalism - can be an excuse to say little, but 'less is more' is still the concept of the century.
__detroit techno - a new, technological music shaped into being in a ruined, yet somehow undefeated city.
__drum'n'bass - pushing beats, and therefore our minds, into unimaginable realms (reinforced has been our literal guide).
__dark electro - remembering those abstract electronic beats that made us contort our bodies in the early '80s (kraftwerk has never been boring to us)
__house - the deep sounds of soulful house music move us, whether they be chicago's sparser, traxier side; disco-based funkifications; or new york's jazzy, sophisticated club house.
__noise - john cage may not have been our literal guide but we hear the sounds all around us as music...and a layer of sonic grunge can be a marvelous thing.
