The End Of An Era

As this August began and the summer was winding down, we had to say some goodbyes.

Goodbye to long-time ele mentals Kevin Ginger and Mira Abellera, who were leaving for Portland, Oregon. Goodbye, temporarily, to Charles, who was hoping to leave soon for the UK.

And, because everyone was leaving, we said goodbye to the house, too. Not just a place to live (it barely qualified for that!), it was ele mental HQ, party place, record library, DJ practice area, and gear storage for four years running.

But these were all happy goodbyes: our friends were moving on to bigger and better things. And we had a lot of good times to celebrate.

So of course we invited everybody over for a party! And took lots of pictures.
