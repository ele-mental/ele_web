21-22 corporation · the mission

 

The 21-22 Corporation is an interdisciplinary entity exploring music, design, and performance. Since 1992, the Corporation has been at the forefront of experimentation, releasing a wide range of products emphasizing the affinities between electronic music production and innovative design aesthetics. In 2000, the Corporation's uniquely packaged CD compilation "Ele_ments of Light" garnered a Judges' Choice Award from world-renowned designer Matt Owens, of One9ine/VolumeOne.

Over the years, the 21-22 Corporation and its parallel concept, ele_mental, have also been involved in many inter-media events, exploring the grey areas between sound, film, video, sculpture, installation art, computer-mediated environments, and contemporary dance.
