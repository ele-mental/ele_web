who makes music. . .



+ charles noel:
as monochrome: smoov breaks. as Cr02: ambient/acid jazz. also does jungle as fuzzy logic?! and 9th circle

+ titonton (titonton duvante):
smoov techno, sometimes ambient/classical (seriously!)

+ placebo (kevin krahel):
house, traxx (he just started but is getting good!)

+ powerhouse (kevin kennedy):
electro, techno (pounding bass, bruthah)

+ evolution control committee (the high evolutionary):
if you havent heard the committee's music then you are missing out. imagine herb alpert and public enemy, crossbred with brian eno and lenny dee (the organist, not industrial strength gabba), then filtered through einstuerzende neubauten and skinny puppy, all regurgitated and sometimes with a polka beat. EXTREMELY interesting and amusing, fun for the whole family!

+ aaron shinn:
as ghost or citizen: experimental jungle...very tight, very evil...waaay out there

+ m/c/jones (chris jones):
traxxy stuff, sometimes ambient

+ kingSize (stephen hindman):
jungle, ragga jungle...great beatwork, mate!

+ todd sines:
as .x trak and trait: minimal bleepy traxx. as enhanced and neriqs: smooth/ambient techno

+ ihannoah (rich ratvasky):
really cool experimental tribal/techno stuff

+ myungho choi:
as elliptical: melodic techno-traxx. as spore: melodic ambient

+ porch (andrew tweed):
dry, wry, classically-influenced techno

+ gAgA:
yes, a BAND, no less...featuring mark gunderson (of the evolution control committee), gary xaoui (of lyceum 23), karen kreutzfeld (nerak), edward luna (lunar), and often assisted by elliot 12trees. imagine the tribal crashing of crash worship, but less serious. MUCH less serious.

+ a.mbience r.ecovery s.ystem (todd sines & charles noel):
improvisatory ambient soundscapes

+ kevin ginger:
weird hardtraxx.

+ others...
