name::  almira abellera

aliases: : mira, the big M, bionix

specialties:: trainspotting,  e-e-electro , head collator (21/22 corporation), cookin' shit up.

 

 

info:: 

mira abellera first felt the resonant forces of ele-mental during the second *snow* event - snowball (winter 1996). performances by home-town legends todd sines and <black and blue> (titonton and monochrome), and entertaining antics by detroit's heckle and jeckle (paris and billeebob) kept the packed rooms jumping. she has since taken a more active role with ele-mental and 21/22 as logistical support staff -- helping out with the records and events.

her love of the music took root while listening to the radio growing up in cincinnati, ohio. while the only "black" radio station played rhythm and blues during the week, they would mix in flavors of "naked funk" by the likes of Prince, Roger and Zapp, and Laid Back. but it was the saturday night dj who really opened her up to the realm of electronic music. artists ranging from Egyptian Lover, the Art of Noise, and Kraftwerk were mixed in proper. a love of hip-hop and breaks during high-school naturally grew out of her early musical influences.

it was then that a high-school friend, jon marshall of the 5 Deez introduced her to the psychedelic beats and melodies of jazz classics by Bobbi Humphreys and the master Miles Davis. seemed to she saw turntables for the first time and she's been trainspotting ever since.
