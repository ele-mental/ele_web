A B S T R A C T
the funda_mentals______

ele_mental is a loose concept binding many people and ideas together. every person that participates in the concept does so on whatever level they feel comfortable. this includes everything from simply offering moral support, encouragement, and/or attendance at our events, to doing music, playing records and sharing music, helping set up or clean up after an event, giving time for a good discussion, allowing the use of equipment/space/sound/time, or simply sharing visions of what the future can hold for us in our new technology-dependent era.

who exactly is IN ele_mental? that is hard to answer, as it is not a "club," nor an official company; there are no formalities nor "membership." it is simply an elaboration of interconnected ideas by those of us who have met on various levels: whether it be in the realms of music, art, image synthesis, graphic design, event coordination, use of computers/technology, history, or through the simple bonds of community and friendship that arise from attending our parties and keeping minds and eyes and ears wide open.

a nucleus does exist, however--the people who have been part of it the longest. but many new people have come along to share in the vision. these are our inner electrons and outer electrons. and because you have read this, you are now part of it too, as a free electron.
