n u c l e u s::
name::
doug holmes

aliases::
"doughboy," "loverboy," "hardcoah mothafucka"

turntable specialties::
hard traxx, technotraxx, gabber, club techno, occasional jungle, occasional slow-breaks (for the kiddies).

other specialties::
founded new production entity called pitch control productions. infiltrated club MEKKA (as sunday night resident for 1.5 years), also presided over MAXWELL'S HOUSE weekly wednesday night event at MAXWELL'S. known for being extremely sexy and always ready to go. can also be a bit hyper and absentminded...now what was i saying?
info::
well it is a well-known fact that doug grew up on heavy metal and was a skater for a while, he's also been part of the cru just about as long as anyone can remember. doug basically just started showing up consistently at BODY RELEASE gigs and helped out in whatever way he could. eventually this led him to DJing, and in fact doug was one of the first ele_mentals to start spinning seriously, and he bought DJ equipment very early on (many of us learned on this system). most of his early purchases consisted of gabber and jungle, but eventually he became more interested in hard trax.

at some point doug decided to get his own event together, which ended up being RED TEMPEST in may of 1995. he went on to throw GENERATOR in april 1996 in much the same vein, and after a lot of work he became the resident DJ at mekka, hosting the sunday night techno night for a year and a half. recently, doug was be found in charge of a wednesday night techno night at maxwell's, and has most recently established his own production entity called pitch control productions, to continue his exploration of the traxxier/techno side of dance music.
