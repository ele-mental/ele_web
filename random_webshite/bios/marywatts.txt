In 1994, Mary became involved with the rave scene, first by assisting Rob
Rage in his events (including BIOSPHERE in Oct. 1994), and then by
branching off into her own promotional concept, called ZAP, with whom she
would produce several high-profile events in 1995, such as ONE and
ULTRAVIOLET.  Due to the rigors of event-promotion, Mary decided to leave
that world behind and concentrate on smaller-scale ideas.  She began to
learn to DJ and quickly became confident of her abilities, spinning the 
mushroomy sounds of jazzy house and trip-hop at Basso Bean cafe for
