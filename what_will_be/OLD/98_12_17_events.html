friday 11 december


FIREXIT 6: a separate spectacle

art / performance / music event featuring the atmospheric film "dutch harbor, where the sky breaks its back"
with live improvised score by THE BOXHEAD ENSEMBLE, including members of TORTOISE (among other bands), and a special appearance by famed avant-garde guitarist JIM O'ROURKE.

also featuring electronic elements by TWINE (kent, OH), BAMBULE COMMANDO (praxis/signalfuries), and gallery sounds by the ele_mentals.

admission only $5.00. event starts early at 8:00 PM, film scheduled to start around 11 PM.

DETAILED DIRECTIONS:
from OSU area take high st S to downtown, go R on broad
follow broad st W for 2 miles, to central ave, go R
follow central ave N for 1/2 mile, to mckinley ave, go R
follow mckinley ave for 2 blocks to 1221, space is on the R.

every monday

MENTAL free, weekly event focusing on abstract, ambient, downtempo, trip-hop, techno, house, drum&bass, lounge, noise, post-classical, post-rock, and experimental electronic music

featuring a rotating roster of DJs and live performers. hosted by LUNAR, ANGULAR, & JOHN PHILIP SUICIDE.

10.30 PM - 2.30 AM, located at THE SUGAR SHACK, 1950 n. 4th st. (2 blocks n of 17th ave.)

free admission

[the sugar shack is now also the home of meetings to discuss the state of underground music and art culture in columbus. the meetings focus on the efforts of FIREXIT, MORE THAN MUSIC, and ELE_MENTAL, and start around 9 PM. all are welcome.]
