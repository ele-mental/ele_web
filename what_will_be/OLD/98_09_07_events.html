>>W E . A R E . B A C K<<
"reports of our death have been greatly exaggerated..."

did we say we were quitting?? well, we lied! or, to be more precise, ed luna lied. in short, we all agreed that ele_mental shouldn't just have one architect...it should be built by many. that's why ed, as he has been saying lately, "fired himself" as head of the project. we want to make the infrastructure more accessible. ele_mental belongs to everyone...

if you want to know more, read ed's explanation of why he felt it was necessary to "fire himself" and declare "the end of ele_mental." ed also explains some of the reasons why we've had to break down to re-invent ourselves.
