RESIDUE
DOWN DEEP production's special event went on a short hiatus due to management problems at the SMILING RHINO. regrettably, the rhino is no more, so the event will soon become a weekly event at AXIS, starting wednesday october 6th. MENTAL
free, weekly event focusing on abstract, ambient, downtempo, trip-hop, techno, house, drum&bass, lounge, noise, post-classical, post-rock, and other sundry notions

featuring a rotating roster of DJs and live performers. hosted by LUNAR, IF, & PANTSHEAD (mark gunderson).

11 PM - 2.30 AM, located at INSOMNIA cafe, 1728 n high st
free admission
