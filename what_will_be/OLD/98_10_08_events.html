updated 8 oct 98
>>N E W S<<

well, charles noel is off in birmingham, supposedly "studying," and another DARKSIDE event looks like it's going to happen (organized by ed luna and gordon sharp). that's the obvious news.

but there are deeper changes in the works. tony ramos has been working on getting us a proper mailing list which will update everyone on our upcoming events and other news. expect that to be up and running within the next few weeks.

also, we have added the entire ele_mental crew to our communal address, element@pico.concourse.com. when you write to this address, about 10 of us receive it, so it's a great way to get ahold of us all at one time.

also, if you haven't been paying attention lately, ele_mental has undergone some serious changes in the last few months. to find out more, read ed luna's (lengthy) explanation about "the end of ele_mental."
