june 25, 26, 27
ele_mental at ComFest

ele_mental is making a return appearance at this year's ComFest event! last year's setup went so well, and we had so much fun, that it was bound to happen again. everyone will be representin' this time, playing all kinds of music, and some are even attempting live performances (hey, someone's gotta offset the rock-heavyness of ComFest, so why not us?).

if you're in the mood to be outside, and want to hang out, and you feel like hearing some great music, this is the weekend to do it.

as always, the location for the Community Festival is goodale park, between high st and neil ave in the short north area. this year, we plan on setting up next to the playground near the main stage, under a big tree (southwest corner of the park). it's the same location we used on the last day of the fest last year.

so far, we plan to be there every day of the event:
- friday from 6 PM - 11 PM
- saturday from 1 PM - 11 PM
- sunday from 1 PM - 11 PM

Live PA Schedule will be posted soon.

If you want to help in any way, just show up at the park on time and look for someone wearing an ele_mental sticker. Volunteering to help the ComFest organizers is GREATLY appreciated. Keep in mind that parking is a premium - plan to carpool!
