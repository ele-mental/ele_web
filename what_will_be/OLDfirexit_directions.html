DIRECTIONS TO FIREXIT:

- from OSU area take high st S to downtown, find broad st and take a R
- follow broad st W for 2 miles, to central ave (look for u-haul on the R)
- go R on central and follow it N for 1/2 mile, to mckinley ave
- go R on mckinley and follow it for 2 blocks to 1221, space is on the R.


- from out-of-town find I-70 and get of at exit #97 (w broad st), approximately 2 miles W of downtown columbus.
- take broad 2 blocks back towards downtown and find central ave (a u-haul will be on your L).
- go L on central and follow N for 1/2 mile, to mckinley ave
- go R on mckinley and follow it for 2 blocks to 1221, space is on the R.
