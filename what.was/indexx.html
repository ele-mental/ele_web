from WHAT:

 "ele mental began in may 1993 as a loose, undefined affiliation of people brought together by their involvement in the (modest) electronic music culture of columbus, ohio."

"as an event-throwing entity, ele_mental started with the notion of creating affordable dance music parties with an experimental and artistic edge. over the past 5 years, we have thrown over 30 events--from the biggest mega-raves to some of the smallest, most experimental underground events of the midwest region.

ele_mental has existed "officially" since may 18, 1993, with our first event entitled ELE_MENTAL. as a concept, ele_mental grew out of friendships that had been established in the period from late 1991 to early 1993.

a detailed history of our activities may seem like a self-serving thing to do, and dwelling on the past can be useless unless it is to find new inspirations for the future. but this short history (which i hope many others will add to) is here to document our experiment so far, and to educate those who want more details about what we have tried to do in the past.

thanks to everyone (from columbus and beyond) who has gotten us here and those yet to come...
